About the Studio

Where Craft
Meets Care

RenneLuxNails was founded in Texas with one goal: deliver a nail experience that respects your time, your style, and your nails' health. We use only premium, non-toxic products — nothing less.

Every artist on our team holds a professional certification and trains continuously. We sterilise all tools between every client, every time — no exceptions.

  • Non-toxic, premium-grade products only
  • Full tool sterilisation between every client
  • Free correction within 5 days of appointment
  • Private, appointment-only studio
